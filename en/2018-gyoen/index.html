Gyoen in 2018

Dec 20, 2018

I go to Shinjuku Gyoen several times each year. It’s a place where I can enjoy the change of seasons, something that can be difficult to experience in urban areas.

The cherry blossoms in spring, the prairies of summer, and the maple leaves during autumn each have their own charm. However, in January, I was able to view a world of white—a snowscape—when snow was falling heavily.
